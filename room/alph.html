blackbird


the lights immediately turn on, and
you see a funny creature made out of
entirely letters from the alphabet
alph!
balph!
calph!
do you like our names?
ever since we were born,
from the day we were conceived,
good names, great names!
how do I get out of here? you interject.
I don't know.
just try the door.
knowing you has been a pleasure!
later, alligator.
